KYDW Undergraduate Academic Team

Founded in 2023 · Northeastern University MBIE

Welcome

KYDW, founded in December 2023, is an undergraduate research team initiated by students from the School of Biomedical Engineering, Northeastern University. Currently, the team has 61 members, distributed across Northeastern University, Fudan University, Tongji University, Liaoning University, Soochow University, Beijing Jiaotong University, and other institutions. We aim to unlock the research potential and interest of undergraduates, providing strong academic support for their graduate school applications and future research careers.

About Us

Research ability is one of the most competitive advantages in undergraduate admissions, often serving as a key factor for entering top universities. KYDW is dedicated to equipping undergraduates with strong research support and cultivating their academic curiosity and creativity.

The first cohort of members (Class of 2021) has successfully advanced to institutions such as the University of Cambridge (PhD), Peking University (PhD), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (MPhil), Zhejiang University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (PhD), and Peking University Health Science Center (PhD).

Activities

New member training(Long Term)

We hold intensive recruitment and training sessions each semester to help new members quickly adapt to academic research and teamwork.

Requirements: First- to third-year undergraduates from any university and major who are interested in research.

Bio - AI Workshop(Long Term)

Weekly workshops focus on the intersection of biology and artificial intelligence, encouraging interdisciplinary exchange and innovation.

Speaker Requirements: Postgraduate (PhD/MPhil/MRes) or equivalent (undergraduate/MSC program graduate students).

Audience: Any interested student.

Northeastern University MBIE Planning Lecture (2025.10)

This lecture will provide students from Northeastern University's School of Medical Engineering with an introduction to relevant career development, sharing cutting-edge developments and past experiences.

Speaker: Liu Hanyu (Founder and Leader of KYDW).

Audience: Any interested student.

Collaborative Projects(Long Term)

For students with established research capabilities, we collaborate on specialized projects based on the current tasks of both the student and the team, aiming to achieve mutual benefit.

Collaborator requirements: Published papers (For SCI Q2 journals or CCF B or lower, we require the collaborator to be listed first in the publication).

Fudan University Undergraduate Research Skills Training(2025.9-12)

This is a pilot project jointly launched by the University of Cambridge and Fudan University to cultivate undergraduates into future research stars. We will share the disseminable aspects of this project, focusing on research methods and practical skills, as well as practical projects in medical artificial intelligence.

Audience: Any interested student.
